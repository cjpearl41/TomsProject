About Us


Our History


My high school was old and run down. It was an old school and it didn't come close to comparing with the newer schools. That's why I started this company, to help update the older schools with current renovations. Founded in 2010, we aim to bring old schools back to life.


Our Mission

Our mission is to help revitalize the old schools that get out classed by the newer ones. We aim to renovate the older schools so that they are more modern and are not so outclassed by the modern schools. Old schools have history in them and we want to keep that history in tact while we build the school up.
